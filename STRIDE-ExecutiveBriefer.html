Executive Briefer

I. Purpose & Strategic Vision

The Strategic Resource Inventory for Deployment Efficiency (STRIDE) Dashboard is the Department of Education's (DepEd) cloud-based analytics hub. Developed to solve the longstanding challenge of fragmented information, STRIDE consolidates human resource, organizational development, and infrastructure data into a single, unified platform.

"Our primary goal is to transform raw records into actionable, holistic intelligence, ensuring that resource deployment decisions are data-driven and precise."

II. Core Capabilities & Key Features

STRIDE provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed to visualize critical resources and automate reporting workflows across all administrative levels.

Education Resource Dashboard A central visualization tool featuring interactive charts and graphs that analyze critical resources like classroom inventory, teacher shortages, and enrollment figures.
Hierarchical Drill-Down Allows users to navigate from a broad regional overview down to the division, municipality, district, and granular data of an individual school.
Multivariable Analysis Enables decision-makers to cross-reference compounding variables—such as plotting "Classroom Shortage" against "Enrollment"—to uncover complex correlations and root causes of systemic inefficiencies.
Geospatial Mapping & Locator Uses advanced technology to plot school locations on an interactive map. This provides spatial context, helping stakeholders visualize the urban versus rural divide and monitor congestion.
Plantilla Positions View A dedicated module for tracking human resources, displaying authorized items versus filled and unfilled positions across divisions.
Automated Report Generation Reactive data tables that update instantly and allow users to convert dashboard insights into downloadable CSV, Excel, or PDF reports for offline analysis.

III. Strategic Management of Classroom Shortages

The Philippines currently faces the challenge of addressing classroom shortage, and STRIDE functions as a tool for monitoring and addressing this backlog.

1. Precise Targeting & Allocation

By integrating enrollment, staffing, and infrastructure data, STRIDE allows regional and division offices to see exactly where classrooms are most needed and deploy resources accordingly.

2. Quick Search and Mapping

STRIDE allows users to quickly search for schools and their respective locations within the platform, allowing for quick evaluation and assessment of classroom and infrastructure needs.

3. Advanced Analytics

Through its Advanced Analytics feature, users can look deeper into the classroom shortage problem by compounding multiple variables and determine specific schools that highly need urgent assistance.
